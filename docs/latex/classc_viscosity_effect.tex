\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Creates a velocity-\/dependent (viscous) field. 

Apply a velocity-\/dependent resistive or assistive force to the haptic device. This is done through a 3x3 matrix $B$

\[ B=\begin{bmatrix} v_x & v_{xy} & v_{xz} \\ v_{xy} & v_y & v_{yz} \\ v_{xz} & v_{yz} & v_z \end{bmatrix} \] where each element of $B$ is a multiplier for the velocity of the haptic tool in that dimension. Thus, resistive forces in the {\itshape x-\/direction} only would have negative values in $v_x$, while nonzero values in the off-\/diagonals will produce a curl field.
